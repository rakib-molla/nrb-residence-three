About Us

NRB Residence is a new kind of real estate platform in Dhaka, created to make owning land or an apartment easier and more accessible for everyone. We offer plots and beautifully located apartments at prices people can realistically afford. Anyone can explore and buy their property through a simple digital process, without long paperwork or complicated steps.

Our residential projects are designed for comfortable living with all the essential digital services that modern families expect. With installment plans starting from only BDT 10,000, we make it possible for more people to begin their journey toward owning a home. We take legal matters very seriously and ensure every plot is fully verified and safe to purchase. Most importantly, we are committed to delivering your property on time.
